2012

The best year of trickshoting.

Yes, 2012 gets it's own page.

2012 was a huge year. After finding increasingly great success in 2011 the trickshoting community was far from finished growing. Right from the start of 2012 the shots themselves began to improve. More spins were added. 360's were no longer considered up to standards and the cleanliness and fluidity of the tricks were greatly improved. Trickshots no longer looked like they were choppy and this made the community fall even further in love with the videos being produced. Teams like FaZe began to acquire hundreds of thousands of views on every single video and the previous mentioned illcams series often flirting with 1 million views. Fall of 2012 trickshoting on Modern Warfare 2 had reached it's peak as FaZe Clan had reached 1 million subscribers surpassing OpTic Gaming and becoming the largest gaming organization on YouTube, all from spinning in circles on a war game. Crazy.

The End of the Modern Warfare 2 Era.

November had dawned on us all and the new Call of Duty game had come out. The two prior titles Call of Duty Black Ops 1, and Call of Duty Modern Warfare 3 failed to dethrone Modern Warfare 2 as the trickshoting staple in 2010 and 2011. However the new release Call of Duty Black Ops 2 was a different story entirely. At first, Black Ops 2 seemed as though it would suffer the same fate as the original black ops and everyone would slowly transition back to Modern Warfare 2.
